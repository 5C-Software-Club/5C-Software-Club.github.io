What is this club?

5C Software is a brand new club with the mission of helping aspiring developers apply what they have learned in CS classes and personal experience to develop their own real-world projects. We hope to foster a community of innovative, driven, and diverse creators to help bring project ideas into fruition. We have meetings every month, featuring speakers and tutorials, as well as support for personal projects.

What we do

  • Code

    We are a beginner-friendly community of programmers who like to learn.

  • Projects

    Members are able to join and start projects through the club. Have a good idea? We offer the support to make it a reality.

  • Workshops

    Each meeting, we have several short talks that introduce new concepts. We also offer interactive workshops covering a vast array of topics.
